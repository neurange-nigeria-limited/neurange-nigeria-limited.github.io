Why we're built this way

Most vendors specialise in one thing. We were incorporated to be trusted with four.

Growing businesses in Nigeria rarely have a single, simple procurement need — a retailer needs branded packaging one month and bulk detergent the next; a distributor needs a trading partner who also understands production. Neurange Nigeria Limited was formed under the Companies and Allied Matters Act 2020 specifically to remove that friction: one CAC-registered entity, one commercial relationship, four disciplines working from the same standard of accountability.

Every engagement — whether it's a print run, a detergent order, a trading contract or a merchandise supply line — moves through the same governance, the same documentation, and the same people who signed the company into existence.

Our registered activities

Four divisions. One standard of delivery.

Each division is licensed under our CAC-registered principal business activity and run by the same accountable leadership.

Trading

Sourcing, import-adjacent supply, and bulk commercial trading relationships for businesses that need a dependable counterparty — with clean invoicing and CAC-verifiable paperwork behind every transaction.

Printing Press

Commercial print production for packaging, branded stationery, and promotional material — built for businesses that need consistent, reorder-ready output rather than one-off jobs.

Detergent Production

Manufactured cleaning products supplied at volume to retailers, institutions and distributors, with formulation and output managed under our registered production activity.

General Merchandise

Flexible supply of everyday goods for shops, offices and household distribution — the catch-all line that lets partners consolidate smaller, varied orders into one relationship.
